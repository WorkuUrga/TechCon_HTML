History

TechCon® North America was first held in 1996 in New Orleans, Louisiana. It has since moved about the U.S., accommodating the growing number of delegates and utility hosts.

Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to connect the most innovative minds in technology while also promoting information sharing. The conference's objectives are to give business executives, entrepreneurs, and tech aficionados a stage on which to participate in thought-provoking talks, exhibit state-of-the-art goods, and establish unrivaled networking opportunities.

Past Speakers

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg is the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Facebook and the founder of LeanIn.Org, a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering women to achieve their ambitions.

Contributions to the Tech Industry:

  • Leadership at Facebook: Growing Facebook's revenue from advertising, developing user engagement tactics, and expanding the company globally are just a few of the major projects that Sandberg has been in charge of.
  • Advocacy for Gender Equality: Sandberg has been a prominent proponent of workplace gender equality, advocating for laws and procedures that assist women in positions of leadership.
  • Digital Skills Initiatives: Sandberg has supported initiatives to improve digital literacy and skills development globally, aiming to bridge the digital divide and empower individuals through technology.

Tim Cook

Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple Inc., succeeding Steve Jobs in 2011. Before joining Apple, Cook served as the Chief Operating Officer at the company and previously worked at Compaq and IBM.

Contributions to the Tech Industry:

  • Sustainability Initiatives: Apple has made sustainability a top priority under Cook's direction, pledging to run its business and produce all of its goods using only renewable energy. Cook has pushed Apple to lessen its carbon impact and promoted environmental responsibility.
  • Privacy Advocacy: Cook has been an outspoken supporter of user privacy, stressing the value of safeguarding client information and pushing for more stringent privacy laws around the world.
  • Product Innovation:Cook has overseen the launch of several iconic products, including the iPhone X, iPad Pro, and Apple Watch. His focus on innovation and customer experience has solidified Apple's position as a leader in consumer electronics.

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. He joined Google in 2004 and has held various leadership roles before becoming CEO in 2015.

Contributions to the Tech Industry:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning: Google's AI research and development has been spearheaded by Pichai, who has incorporated machine learning into products including Google Photos, Google Assistant, and Search.
  • Expansion of Google Services: Under Pichai's leadership, Google has expanded its portfolio of services, including the development of Android, Chrome, and Google Cloud Platform, which have become integral parts of the tech ecosystem.
  • Commitment to Privacy and Security: At Google, Pichai has placed a high priority on user privacy and data security, putting policies in place to safeguard user data and improve openness around data practices.
